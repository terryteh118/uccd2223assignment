Alex G.​ - HEAD CHEF

Alex G. is the head chef of Paradise Dining®. He holds more than 30 years of restaurant and catering experience. Upon relocating to Austin in the mid ‘90s, Alex helped open the first Central Market, where he became executive sous chef.
